Welcome to RYV, a platform designed to help users submit complaints and seek help from respective department. Here citizens can raise their problems related to several government bodies.We would like to say that after the user who has been registered can then raise their complaint and wait for a time so that the particular department reacts for it. And the status of the given problem is being shown continuously.Here we are introducing a voting system to vote the problems and if the number of votes for a problem is more then that problem will be given first priority. And hence the problem is solved.
